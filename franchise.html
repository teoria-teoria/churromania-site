What people ask.

  • What does it cost to open a ChurroManía?↓

    Initial investment ranges from $184,450 to $509,000 depending on store format, from a food truck to a full mall store. Refer to Item 7 of our May 2025 FDD for the full breakdown.

  • Where can I open one?↓

    We are actively expanding in Florida, Texas, California, Arizona, Spain, Mexico City, and other Latin American markets. Formats range from Walmart stores and shopping centers to malls and food trucks.

  • How long is training?↓

    Comprehensive training from our churro experts continues past opening day, with coaching across sales, marketing, and production, directly supervised by our founders and CEO.

  • Do I need restaurant experience?↓

    No. Our patented Churro Mix makes the best churros easy to make, and our training is built for operators new to food service. Experience helps, but it is not required.

  • What support do I get?↓

    Site selection, training, supply chain, marketing playbooks, technology, and ongoing operations support. We have refined the playbook since 1997 and share all of it.

  • Can I open multiple units?↓

    Yes. We work with single-unit operators and multi-unit groups. Whether you have 1 or 5 or 100 stores, we help you run yours at its best.

  • What is the average unit volume?↓

    Average unit volume is $584,000, and 37% of our stores are at or above that average. Refer to Item 19 of our May 2025 FDD for more detailed information.

  • How long does it take to open?↓

    From signed agreement to opening day is typically a few months, depending on site, build-out, and permitting in your market.
