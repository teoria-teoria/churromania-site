FAQ

Questions, answered.

What does it cost to open a ChurroManía franchise?+

Total investment depends on the format and market, from a compact kiosk to a full store. We walk you through the full cost breakdown on the discovery call once we understand your territory.

Where can I open one?+

We are actively expanding in Florida, Texas, California, Arizona, Spain, Mexico City, and other Latin American markets. If you have a strong market elsewhere, tell us in the form.

How long is training?+

The core program runs a few weeks and covers operations, the product, and store management. We stay with you through opening and beyond.

Do I need restaurant experience?+

No. Many of our operators are first-timers. The format is simple by design and our training is built for people new to food service. Experience helps, but it is not required.

What support do I get?+

Site selection, training, supply chain, marketing playbooks, technology, and ongoing operations support. We have opened stores on three continents and we share what we have learned.

Can I open multiple units?+

Yes. We work with single-unit operators and multi-unit groups, and we structure area deals for partners who want to build a territory.

What is the typical return?+

Returns vary by market, format, and operator. We share real unit economics from comparable stores during the discovery process so you can model it for yourself.

How long does it take to open?+

From signed agreement to opening day is typically a few months, depending on site, build-out, and permitting in your market.
